PRODUCT DESCRIPTION

Unboxing is the unpacking of products where the process is captured on video and uploaded to the internet. The item is then explained in detail and can sometimes be demonstrated as well. Watching this type of content can fill those curious vibes, and perhaps even encourage the viewer to get their own if they can afford it! Videos include:

  • forgetting what was purchased online
  • struggling to open boxes
  • long drawn out conversations with the camera
  • an overwhelming amount of packages
  • Etc.

Materials used for these type of videos include a set of packaged goods purchased online, a pair scissors or a box knife, and a decent camera and microphone to record.
