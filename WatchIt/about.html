MEET THE CREATOR

Elizabeth Nguyen is a current SJSU student who is currently working to make her way into the DMA program. Inspired by the daily patterns of those around her, Elizabeth created this project to not only test her web-development skills, but to also express her interest in the way society and technology uphold together.





ABOUT THE PROJECT

WATCHIT! was created to mimick any typical online shopping website. Except, rather than selling physical products with monetary value, I wanted to sell "watch-time." In order to work in a timely manner, I generalized topics based on top video trends, took clips from those, and created a series of montage videos to indicate this sort of "package deal" when a viewer chooses to watch whichever one they pick. This is supposed to emphasize the idea that simply watching internet videos has merged itself into a sort of culture that is now a conventional part of our daily lives. So much so that it seems to be affecting the film and television industries, though this can be debated.

This project is still in the works, as there are a lot of different types of growing content to go through.
