PRODUCT DESCRIPTION

Also known as "autonomous sensory meridian response," ASMR is a popular type of content that aims to give viewers a sense of relaxation, often causing a tingling sensation at the back of their head and/or spine. ASMR videos include:

  • Gentle whispering
  • Smacking of the lips
  • Nail tapping/scratching on hard surfaces
  • Brushing sounds
  • Crunching/chewing sounds
  • Etc.
