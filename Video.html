Seamstress

A somewhat eerie perception of female stereotypes. I grew up in a traditional household so I was expected to follow certain standards. There are two videos overlaid on top of each other; one is stock footage from 1955 called Sewing Machine and the other is a montage of short clips I filmed, each scene representing some form of sewing.
