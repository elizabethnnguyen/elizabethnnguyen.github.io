Digitized Being: 3-D Render, Mesh Mixer, Blender, Google Poly, 2019.

A 3-D mash-up piece that utilizes free 3-D assets with designs of my own. Both the monitor and keyboard are free assets from Google Poly and the torso is another asset from a standard human body template from MeshMixer. Attached everything in Blender and added sockets and wires to arms and back.
